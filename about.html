about me

I'm Liev Fletcher, a UI/UX Designer & Front-End Developer hybrid based in Tucson. I love the creative challenges of design, and the technical challenges of development. At the moment, I am looking for work that will allow me to continue to grow in this dynamic position and broaden my current skill set.



What I bring to the table:
  • Code informed designs. My designs are made to streamline the design to development process. Plus, communication with developers is effortless because I understand their process.

  • Empathy. Because every designer needs to put themselves in the user's shoes first and foremost.

  • Thinking outside of the box. Unique/unconventional designs are my favorite thing to do, give me creative space and I will produce something special you've never seen before but will love just the same. I am always looking for inspiration in unexpected places.

  • Coding capabilities. I can be a 2-in1 when it comes to design and development. This saves companies money by merging the two separate positions, and saves time as well.

  • Eagerness to grow. I will never stop seeking knowledge, and in a constantly changing and evolving field, this is essential to keeping up to date with the newest trends and methods. I constantly seek opportunities to learn new skills, because it's boring not to.
